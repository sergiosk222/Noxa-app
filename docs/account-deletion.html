ACCOUNT CONTROL

Delete your NOXA account

You can request permanent deletion of your account and associated personal data. Removing the app from your device does not delete your account.

Last updated July 15, 2026

How to submit a request

  1. Email noxastreetapp@gmail.com from the email address registered to your NOXA account.
  2. Use the subject line Delete my NOXA account.
  3. Include your NOXA username if you have one. Do not send your password.
  4. We will confirm the request and may ask for limited information needed to verify account ownership.
Start deletion request

What will be deleted

  • Your authentication account, profile, avatar, and account identifiers.
  • Your garage vehicles and uploaded vehicle images.
  • Your posts, comments, likes, saves, follows, and other social relationships.
  • Your messages, gallery uploads, invitations, join requests, memberships, poll votes, and convoy participation.
  • Crews, events, and related content owned by your account, where deletion follows the service’s ownership rules.
  • Any active Live Drive location record associated with your account.

Timing and limited retention

Verified requests are normally completed within 30 days. We will remove or de-identify the account and associated personal data from active systems. Limited records may be retained where reasonably necessary for security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or legal compliance. Residual copies may remain temporarily in protected backups until the normal backup cycle expires.

Account deletion is permanent. You may cancel before completion by replying to the request email, but deleted data may not be recoverable once processing is complete.
